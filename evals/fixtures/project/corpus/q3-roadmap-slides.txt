# Q3 Roadmap Review

## Slide 1 — Reliability
- Reduce stuck ingestion jobs below 0.5%.
- Add resumable processing with three retry attempts.

## Slide 2 — Retrieval quality
- Establish bilingual answer and citation baselines.
- Target citation correctness of 95% before general availability.

## Slide 3 — Rollout
- Run a 10% internal shadow deployment in August.
- Expand to a 30% customer preview only after two stable weeks.

## 幻灯片 4 — 明确不做
- 第三季度不更换向量数据库。
- 第三季度不引入自动执行文档指令的能力。
