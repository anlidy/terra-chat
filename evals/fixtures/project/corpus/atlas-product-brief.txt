Atlas Product Brief

Atlas is a document collaboration product for regulated teams. The first public pilot launches on 15 October 2026. The pilot targets legal and pharmaceutical customers in Singapore and London.

The release has three priorities: private document storage, evidence-linked answers, and a complete audit trail. Success means at least 80% of pilot questions receive a verified citation and no document is readable outside its owning workspace.

The team will not ship automated document deletion during the pilot. That capability remains scheduled for the general-availability release.
